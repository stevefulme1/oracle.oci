## Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for:

- Reviewing and merging pull requests
- Triaging and responding to issues
- Managing releases and versioning
- Ensuring CI/CD pipelines remain functional
- Keeping dependencies up to date
- Coordinating security vulnerability responses

## Becoming a Maintainer

Regular contributors who demonstrate sustained, high-quality contributions
may be invited to become maintainers. If you are interested, open an issue
or reach out to an existing maintainer.
